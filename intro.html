The Shadow Syndicate

In the depths of the dark web, a notorious hacking group, known only as Shadow Syndicate, has launched a silent cyber-assault on a major online platform. Their methods are elusive, their traces nearly invisible. But there's one flaw—they always leave a clue.

Your mission is to step into the shoes of a digital detective, a white-hat hacker determined to uncover the vulnerabilities before real damage is done.

The Clues

Rumors suggest that certain file extensions are not properly validated. Can a simple case-change slip past the security measures?

Whispers from the underground say that a cleverly crafted command might just reveal what lies at the root.

A ghost in the system claims that authentication is an illusion. Perhaps a single query holds the key to gaining access?

Identifiers should be unique, yet a minor tweak reveals another's secrets. But what if those identifiers aren’t as random as they seem?

Messages on the black market speak of malicious scripts lurking in the shadows. Some are reflected instantly; others are stored, waiting for the perfect moment to strike.

A hidden API is said to hold a crucial piece of the puzzle. The flag may be right in front of you—wrapped within familiar brackets.

The Challenge

To bring down the Shadow Syndicate, you must uncover their attack vectors. Can you crack the vulnerabilities, expose their exploits, and retrieve the ultimate flag?

Are you ready?
